Follow Usher – The School of Robotics for more updates

Usher is dedicated to inspiring young minds with cutting-edge robotics, programming, and innovation skills. Join our community to learn, create, and grow with expert mentorship and exciting hands-on projects.

Founded with a passion to ignite curiosity and creativity, Usher – The School of Robotics has become a hub for young learners who dream big and build bigger. We provide a nurturing environment where children explore robotics, coding, artificial intelligence, and electronics through engaging, hands-on projects.

Our expert mentors guide students step-by-step, helping them transform ideas into real-world applications. From beginner-friendly courses to advanced challenges, Usher supports every learner’s journey to becoming a confident innovator.

We believe technology education should be fun, inclusive, and empowering — inspiring the next generation of creators and problem-solvers.
