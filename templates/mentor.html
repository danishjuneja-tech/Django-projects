Founder & CEO, Usher – The School of Robotics

Er. Umesh Bajaj is the visionary Founder and CEO of Usher – The School of Robotics. With decades of experience in robotics and engineering, he is dedicated to empowering students through innovative education and hands-on learning. Under his leadership, Usher has become a leading institution fostering creativity, technology skills, and future-ready innovators.

Our Mission

At Usher – The School of Robotics, our mission is to foster an inspiring and inclusive learning environment where students of all ages and backgrounds can discover, learn, and excel in robotics, programming, AI, and electronics. We are dedicated to nurturing creativity, critical thinking, and hands-on skills through expertly designed projects and mentorship.

Our Vision

Our vision at Usher is to become a leading hub for cultivating future innovators and leaders in technology. We envision a community where creativity meets technology, enabling students to harness the power of robotics and AI to solve real-world challenges.
